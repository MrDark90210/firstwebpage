Maxing out the lifespan of produce

  • Banannas
    1. Always pick some ripe bannanas and unripe
    2. Make sure to store the ripe bannanas in the fridge, and unripe on the counter
    3. If you have some really ripe bannanas store them in the freezer!
  • Apples
    1. Apples can be stored on the counter tops or fridge...They are tough!
  • Mangoes
    1. Mangoes are delicate and can ripen really quickly, store them on the countertop similiar to bannanas
    2. Mangoes have a max life span of about 5 days
  • Greek Yogurt!
    1. Always store greek yogurt in the fridge, it can have a max lifespan of about 2 weeks!
  • Cashews
    1. Cashews have the longest life span of about 6 months!
